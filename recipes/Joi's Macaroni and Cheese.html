Joi's Macaroni and Cheese

Kio, Pasta

Source: Mashup

Ingredients

1 pound elbows, shells, cavatappi, farfalle, fusilli or other short, tube-shaped pasta

Kosher salt

2 cups whole milk

¼ teaspoon finely grated nutmeg

4 ounces cream cheese, cut into 1-inch pieces

12 ounces sharp or white Cheddar, grated or 6 ounces Brie, rind removed and cheese cut into chunks and 3 ounces Parmigiano-Reggiano, grated ( 3/4 cup) and 1 cup of mascarpone

3 large eggs, lightly beaten

3 tablespoons unsalted butter

Freshly ground pepper

Directions

Boil paste to al dente.

Meanwhile, bring milk up to a simmer in a large pot. (The pot should be large enough to hold all the pasta when cooked.)

Reduce heat to low, add cream cheese, and whisk until it’s completely blended and no lumps remain.

Add cheddar or brie, mascarpone cheese and butter, whisking until everything is completely melted.

Season with nutmeg, salt and a generous amount of pepper.

Add cooked pasta and stir to coat.

In a separate bowl, whisk together eggs and parmigiano. Stir egg mixture into pasta.

Continue to cook over medium-low heat until the sauce has thickened and is coating each piece of pasta nicely, 2 to 3 minutes; sauce will continue to thicken as it cools. Season again with more salt and pepper before serving.
