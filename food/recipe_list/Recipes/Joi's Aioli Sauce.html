Joi's Aioli Sauce

Sauce

Ingredients

2 cloves of garlic or 1 tablespoon of green garlic

1 tbsp of lemon juice, more to taste

1/2 tsp grated lemon zest

1/4 tsp freshly cracked black pepper

1/2 teaspoon fine sea salt, more to tast

1 large egg

1 large egg yolk

3/4 cup extra-virgin olive oil

1 tablespoon of fresh tarragon or chives


Option:


2 tbsp Dijon mustard

Directions

Put garlic in Thermomix. Mince garlic (speed 7 / 3 sec) wipe down sides with spatula and do twice.

Add lemon juice, salt and pepper and let sit a min or two in Thermomix.

Add egg and yolk, mustard, zest and blend until combined (Speed 6 / 20 sec).

With Thermomix running (speed 4 / 3 min), slowly drizzle oil steadily to create an emulsification.

Season for taste with salt lemon juice.

Stir in tarragon or chives.
